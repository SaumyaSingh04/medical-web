Our Story

Bringing you the purest form of Ayurveda, preserving ancient wisdom for modern lifestyles.

Rooted in 5,000 Years of Tradition

Founded by a lineage of Ayurvedic physicians in Kerala, Triven Ayurveda bridges the gap between ancient herbal wisdom and modern scientific validation. We don't just sell supplements; we formulate holistic remedies designed to treat the root cause, not just the symptoms.

Every product begins its journey in our partner organic farms across the Western Ghats. We strictly adhere to Vrikshayurveda (traditional organic farming) principles — no synthetic pesticides, no chemical fertilisers, only nature's own methods.

Our name "Triven" symbolises the sacred confluence — where ancient tradition, modern science, and pure nature meet to create healing that truly works.
