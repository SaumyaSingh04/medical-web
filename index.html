Understand Your Dosha

In Ayurveda, true health is achieved when your mind-body type (Dosha) is in perfect balance.

Vata

(Air & Space)

Governs movement and creativity. When imbalanced, it causes anxiety, dry skin, and irregular digestion.

Balance Vata

Pitta

(Fire & Water)

Governs metabolism and intellect. When imbalanced, it causes inflammation, heartburn, and irritability.

Balance Pitta

Kapha

(Earth & Water)

Governs structure and strength. When imbalanced, it causes lethargy, weight gain, and congestion.

Balance Kapha
